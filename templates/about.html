About WebHawk

WebHawk is a phishing and fake website detection system designed to protect users by analyzing URL patterns and identifying suspicious websites in real time.

Features:

  • Real-time URL analysis
  • Detection of phishing, typosquatting, and suspicious keywords
  • History of recent URLs checked
  • Dark mode support
  • Simple and responsive UI
